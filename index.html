Instructions:
------------
1. Place your bets.

2. Press Deal Cards to start the match after placing your bets.

3. Press Hit to get another card.

4. Press Stand to end your turn.

5. If you win, you will gain your bet x2 in your bank.

6. If you lose, you lose your bet.

7. Getting 21 (Ace + any card value with 10) on initial hand, you will gain your bet x2 + another 1.5x your bet!

8. If dealer gets 21 and its not a tie then you just lose your bet.

9. Cards: 2,3,4,5,6,7,8,9,10,

10. Jack = 10, Queen = 10, King = 10, Aces will initially be 11 and can be turned to 1.
